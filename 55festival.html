Joy Blossom Market

An AI-powered campaign for 55 Shanghai Shopping Festival

PROJECT TYPE
Professional project
Duration
2 Months
ROLE
Project manager intern
Tools
Figma, Recraft, Stable Diffusion

ABOUT PROJECT

The Shanghai "May 5th Shopping Festival" is a large-scale consumer promotion event organized by the Shanghai Municipal Government, featuring thousands of activities to boost retail and cultural spending, with a focus on international brands and innovative shopping experiences

The "Joy Blossom Market" interactive installation features six meticulously designed thematic scenes, using "flowers" as a medium and leveraging AI-powered real-time interaction to invite visitors on a "floral journey".

USER JOURNEY

The flower journey has six themes, two of which introduce AI-powered interactive touch points.
