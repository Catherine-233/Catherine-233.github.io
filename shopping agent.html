Problem Statement

The architectural design industry has always faced problems such as short design cycles and frequent modifications, and the demand for efficient workflows is very urgent.

Solution Statement

Through the AI ​​architectural auxiliary drawing platform, it brings inspiration to designers, helps to deepen the scheme and compare multiple schemes, and improves the work efficiency of architects and the productivity of construction companies.

RESEARCH

User Survey

Before rolling out the program, we did a survey on people's habbits when using AI products. My role was to find out the young generation's behavioral patterns when using AI services.

AI has been deeply integrated into the life of the young generation.
The penetration rate of AI in e-commerce scenarios is low, and there is a lot of room for growth.
Most users still focus on using general AI tools instead of vertical AI APPs.
Most of them mainly passively receive AI-related information, so user habits need to be cultivated.

I then narrow my focus to our business in shopping APPs and live streaming platforms

GOAL

The Agent System

Live-streaming agent
  • AI understands the host's introduction and highlights key information for users to view.
  • After the livestream ends, a structured index and interactive progress bar are generated, allowing users to review content of interest.
  • AI automatically generates highlight moments to supplement the product detail page introduction.
Searching agent
  • AI processes users' vague descriptions or specific requests and matches them with relevant products.
Customer service agent
  • AI interprets the text and images on the product detail page and answers user questions.

Function Prototype

Coze workflows and Python code

I utilize coze to create a prototype of the live-streaming agent. It can automatically generate a structured index ofthe host's introduction, and split the video according to the index.

For the customer service agent, I called Deepseek's API and utilized OCR to acheive the function of extracting text from images.

When it comes to the searching agent, I prepared a structured database of items so that LLM can easily gather information.

IP character

Shanghai OCJ Shopping Agent

I designed an IP character for the shopping platform, reminding users to user AI functions at different stages of the shopping process. The Shanghai TV Official Channel is often nick named as "the tomato TV", so the character is designed as a tomato.
